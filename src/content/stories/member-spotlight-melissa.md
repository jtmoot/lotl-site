This week we are cheering on Melissa, who picked up a club for the very first
time this spring and has not missed a Monday since. 👏

When Melissa registered she told us she was nervous about slowing everyone down.
A few lessons with our pro Christian Grace later, she was draining putts and
laughing the whole way around. That is the whole point of this league. You do not
need to be good at golf to belong here. You just need to show up and have fun.

Melissa, thank you for letting us share your story. Here is to a great season.
